/disable-lessons

Call the `postmortem_disable_lessons` plugin tool with flags mapped from $ARGUMENTS. Examples:

tool: postmortem_disable_lessons
tool: postmortem_disable_lessons --enable
tool: postmortem_disable_lessons --json
